## Design Context

### Users
PodSum.cc is used by people who collect long-form podcast or video transcripts and turn them into reusable summaries, notes, and knowledge assets. The primary daily workflow is scanning an existing summary library, filtering private/public/processing items, opening a summary, and uploading the next transcript.

### Brand Personality
Quiet, useful, and knowledge-focused. The interface should feel like a calm workspace rather than a marketing page: fast to scan, trustworthy, and a little refined.

### Aesthetic Direction
Use the existing warm paper palette: beige background, ivory panels, muted tan borders, deep sage green actions, and dark brown text. Avoid loud gradients, oversized hero layouts, decorative blobs, and crowded footer navigation. Header controls should be compact and consistent across the app.

### Design Principles
- The homepage is the main workspace, not a landing page. `My Summaries` is a first-class view/filter and must remain visible.
- Account, credits, pricing, MCP, extension, about, and sign-out belong in the right-side account menu with short labels.
- The day/night theme toggle is a persistent top-right control and must not disappear during navigation redesigns.
- `Upload` is the primary global action; keep it visible and visually stronger than secondary navigation.
- Footer navigation should stay minimal; product operations belong in the header or page body.
